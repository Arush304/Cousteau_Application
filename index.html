ABOUT ME

I possess that important quality of empathy, am a good communicator and an even better listener. I have an amiable yet persuasive style which will help me in this role. Furthermore, I am extremely enthusiastic and passionate and am keen to make my House experience, and crucially the experience of my peers a very memorable one. My strengths are aligned with the key qualities that embody the spirit of Cousteau. The Cousteau house captain needs to be a respectful but also high-spirited and kind-hearted person and I believe that I possess all of these qualities that are ultimately crucial to success as house captain. Additionally, I am open to criticism, and I am constantly looking to improve myself and the house program so that it becomes something that everyone can always enjoy.

I aim to take Cousteau to greater heights and a position of responsibility will help me in that endeavor. I am confident that I can excel in this role which involves motivating my peers and also inspiring them by example. I am ready to openly listen to all suggestions and help improve the house experience for my fellow students. Additionally, I believe that this experience to work with such a bright and diverse set of students will help me enhance my leadership skills.

I have passionately taken part in many house events such as the house water polo, swim gala (despite not being the best swimmer!), and many activities on sports day (only to name a few). I also suggested the Kazoos for 10SCW, so that we could participate in House Music Ensemble and look forward to continuously represent my house in additional events. I will encourage my classmates to share the enthusiasm that I feel for Cousteau and motivate them to participate in events, be open-minded and try new things.
